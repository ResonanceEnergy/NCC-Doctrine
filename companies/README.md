# Companies Directory

## Purpose
Individual company divisions, subsidiaries, and business units within the NCC enterprise ecosystem.

## Ownership
- **Primary Owner**: NCC Corporate Holdings
- **Secondary Owner**: Individual Company CEOs
- **Maintenance Lead**: Corporate Integration Team

## Directory Structure
- `/companies/` - Main companies directory
  - Individual company subdirectories for each NCC subsidiary
  - Company-specific documentation, procedures, and operations
  - Integration protocols and cross-company frameworks

## Company Divisions
- **Augmented_Arbitrage_Corp**: Financial trading and arbitrage operations
- **BiotechCorporation**: Biotechnology research and development
- **Faraday_Financial_Corp**: Financial services and banking
- **Hedge_Fund**: Alternative investment management
- **InnovationLabsDivision**: Research and development
- **MediaCorp**: Media and communications
- **QuantumComputingDivision**: Quantum technology development

## Maintenance Procedures
- **Quarterly Reporting**: All companies submit quarterly performance reports
- **Integration Reviews**: Annual integration audits and optimization
- **Resource Allocation**: Corporate resource allocation and budgeting cycles
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Ongoing compliance with corporate standards
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Cross-company best practices and lessons learned

## Access Control
- **Read Access**: Authorized corporate personnel
- **Write Access**: Company leadership and corporate integration team
- **Execute Access**: Company operations personnel
