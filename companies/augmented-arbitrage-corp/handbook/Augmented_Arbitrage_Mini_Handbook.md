## Page 3: Arbitrage Fundamentals
**What is Arbitrage?** The simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from price differences.

**Types We Execute:**
- **Statistical Arbitrage**: Mean-reversion between correlated assets
- **Triangular Arbitrage**: Currency exchange inefficiencies
- **Options Arbitrage**: Put-call parity violations
- **Cross-Market Arbitrage**: Price discrepancies between exchanges

**Edge Creation:** Technology + Speed + Capital + Risk Management
