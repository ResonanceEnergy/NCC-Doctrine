## SCORING METHODOLOGY

### Funding Availability (25 points max)
- **20-25 pts**: Active DFI programs + govt subsidies + climate finance + MFI sector
- **15-19 pts**: 2-3 funding channels available
- **10-14 pts**: Limited funding (1-2 sources)
- **0-9 pts**: Minimal funding infrastructure

### Terrain Suitability (20 points max)
- **16-20 pts**: Abundant sites (1000+), 3-15m head, year-round flow >100 L/s
- **11-15 pts**: Good availability (100-1000 sites)
- **6-10 pts**: Moderate (10-100 sites)
- **0-5 pts**: Limited terrain opportunities

### Use Case Strength (20 points max)
- **16-20 pts**: Multiple high-value applications (agri-processing, irrigation, off-grid, eco-tourism)
- **11-15 pts**: 2-3 strong use cases
- **6-10 pts**: 1-2 use cases
- **0-5 pts**: Weak demand/market

### Environmental Favorability (15 points max)
- **12-15 pts**: Low ecological sensitivity, existing infrastructure, strong regulatory framework
- **8-11 pts**: Moderate sensitivity with clear mitigation path
- **4-7 pts**: High sensitivity but manageable
- **0-3 pts**: Protected areas, endangered species, regulatory barriers

### Development Readiness (20 points max)
- **16-20 pts**: Strong governance, minimal corruption, established energy sector, easy permits
- **11-15 pts**: Moderate governance, some bureaucracy
- **6-10 pts**: Challenging but improving
- **0-5 pts**: Weak institutions, high corruption, difficult permitting

**Total Score**: 100 points max
