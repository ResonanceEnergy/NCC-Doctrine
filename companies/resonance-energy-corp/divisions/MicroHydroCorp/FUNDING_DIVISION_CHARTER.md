## ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

### **Leadership**
- **Head of Funding:** Senior role; 10+ years project finance/impact investing; DFI relationships; blended finance structuring.
- **Reporting:** Direct to CEO; dotted line to CFO.

### **Team (Build to 5–8 FTE by Year 2)**

**1. DFI & Multilateral Relations (1–2 FTE)**
- **Responsibilities:** IFC, ADB, AfDB, IDB, EBRD, KfW, JICA relationships; pipeline management; proposal development; compliance.
- **Profile:** Ex-DFI staff or consultant; fluent in DFI processes (E&S, procurement, reporting).

**2. Impact Equity & VC (1–2 FTE)**
- **Responsibilities:** Series A/B fundraising; impact investor outreach (Acumen, responsAbility, etc.); pitch decks; due diligence coordination; investor relations.
- **Profile:** VC/growth equity background; impact investing networks; startup fundraising experience.

**3. Grant & Concessional Finance (1 FTE)**
- **Responsibilities:** Bilateral aid (USAID, DFID, GIZ, AFD); climate funds (GCF, CIF, GEF); foundation grants; proposal writing; compliance/reporting.
- **Profile:** Grant writing expert; NGO/development sector experience; climate finance knowledge.

**4. Carbon & Biodiversity Markets (1 FTE)**
- **Responsibilities:** Carbon credit development (Gold Standard, Verra); biodiversity credit exploration; MRV design; forward sales; broker relationships.
- **Profile:** Carbon market specialist; project developer certification experience; remote sensing/MRV technical skills.

**5. Local/MFI Finance (1 FTE, expand per country)**
- **Responsibilities:** MFI partnerships; coop/community financing structures; paygo/lease templates; local banking relationships; currency hedging.
- **Profile:** Microfinance background; emerging market banking; community finance models.

## KEY FUNCTIONS

### **1. Capital Strategy & Roadmap**
- Maintain phase-by-phase capital plan (seed → Series A → growth → infrastructure debt).
- Model capital needs vs deployment targets; cash-flow projections; scenario planning.
- Advise on optimal capital structure (equity/debt/grant mix) per phase and geography.

### **2. Investor Relations & Fundraising**
- Manage investor pipeline (CRM); regular updates; quarterly reporting.
- Coordinate due diligence (financial, technical, E&S, legal).
- Negotiate term sheets; structure deals; close rounds.

### **3. DFI & Multilateral Engagement**
- Map DFI priorities by geography and sector; align project pipeline to mandates.
- Prepare concept notes, full proposals, E&S frameworks.
- Manage DFI approval cycles (often 12–18 months); maintain compliance.

### **4. Grant & Concessional Finance**
- Monitor grant calendars (RFPs, deadlines); rapid response capability.
- Write compelling proposals (theory of change, M&E frameworks, budgets).
- Manage grant execution (reporting, audits, amendments).

### **5. Carbon & Impact Finance**
- Develop carbon methodologies for microhydro (diesel displacement, avoided grid emissions).
- Engage validators, verifiers; manage certification process.
- Structure forward sales or bundled credit portfolios; negotiate pricing.

### **6. Local Finance Structuring**
- Design paygo/EaaS tariff templates; SPV structures; community equity models.
- Partner with local MFIs, banks, cooperatives; facilitate end-user financing.
- Manage currency/political risk instruments (DFI guarantees, insurance).

### **7. Reporting & Transparency**
- Maintain investor dashboards (portfolio performance, impact KPIs).
- Publish annual impact reports (households, CO₂e, jobs, fish metrics).
- Coordinate audits (financial, E&S, carbon MRV).

## GOVERNANCE & COMPLIANCE

### **Investment Committee**
- **Composition:** CEO, CFO, Head of Funding, independent director (DFI/impact investing background).
- **Mandate:** Approve capital raises >$500K; review terms; ensure alignment with mission/impact.
- **Cadence:** Quarterly + ad-hoc for major decisions.

### **E&S Compliance**
- Adopt IFC Performance Standards as baseline; exceed where feasible.
- Maintain E&S management system (screening, impact assessment, monitoring, grievance mechanism).
- Third-party audits annually for DFI compliance.

### **Financial Reporting**
- IFRS or US GAAP (align to primary investor base).
- Quarterly financials to investors; annual audited statements.
- Impact reporting: households, kWh, CO₂e, jobs, fish metrics (quarterly).

### **Anti-Corruption & Ethics**
- Zero tolerance for bribery/corruption; FCPA/UK Bribery Act compliance.
- Whistleblower hotline; third-party ethics audits.
- Due diligence on all partners (PEP screening, sanctions checks).
