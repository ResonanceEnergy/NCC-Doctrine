# Micro Hydro Corp Mini Handbook

## Overview
Micro Hydro Corp specializes in small-scale hydroelectric power generation, providing clean, renewable energy solutions for off-grid and grid-tied applications.

## Key Components
1. **Site Assessment:** Evaluate water flow, head height, and environmental impact.
2. **System Design:** Select appropriate turbine type (Pelton, Francis, Kaplan) based on site conditions.
3. **Installation:** Professional setup with safety protocols.
4. **Maintenance:** Regular inspections and cleaning.
5. **Monitoring:** Real-time data collection for performance optimization.

## Financial Model
- Initial investment: $5,000 - $50,000 per system
- Payback period: 3-7 years
- Revenue streams: Energy sales, carbon credits, maintenance services

## Safety and Compliance
- Follow local regulations for water usage and environmental protection.
- Implement safety measures for high-voltage components.
