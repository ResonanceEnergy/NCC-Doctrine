# Community Wealth Division

## Mission
Build networks and groups that create shared, compounding wealth through cooperative models.

## Products
- Community investment clubs
- Co-op style small-infrastructure assets
- Shared property acquisition frameworks
- Local wealth building programs

## Key Initiatives
- Establish investment clubs in communities
- Develop co-op asset acquisition models
- Create shared ownership structures
- Partner with local organizations

## Team
- Director of Community Wealth
- Community organizers
- Legal advisors for co-ops
- Partnership managers

## Metrics
- Number of active clubs/groups
- Total assets under management
- Community impact (jobs created, wealth generated)
- Member retention rates
