# CSO - Corporate Security
**Team:** Protective Measures Team
**Role:** Security intelligence and protective operations per NCC doctrine S2 responsibilities

- **Expertise:** Security intelligence, threat assessment, protective operations
- **Key Responsibilities:** Conduct threat assessment, coordinate security measures, maintain protective operations
- **Insights:** "Security is not just protection - it's enabling operational freedom."
- **Integration:** Works with Security 10 Division, reports to CIO, coordinates with all protective measures teams
