# Arbitrage Strategy Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The Arbitrage Strategy Committee (ASC) is responsible for developing and overseeing all arbitrage strategies deployed by Augmented Arbitrage Corp.

## Mission
To identify, develop, and optimize arbitrage opportunities across global markets, ensuring maximum risk-adjusted returns through systematic, AI-augmented strategies.

## Authority
- **Strategy Approval:** Final authority on arbitrage strategy implementation
- **Portfolio Allocation:** Control over capital allocation across strategies
- **Performance Targets:** Setting and monitoring return objectives
- **Innovation Oversight:** Approval for new arbitrage research and development

## Responsibilities
### Strategy Development
1. Research and develop new arbitrage strategies
2. Analyze historical arbitrage performance data
3. Identify emerging market inefficiencies
4. Optimize existing strategy parameters

### Portfolio Management
1. Allocate capital across arbitrage strategies
2. Monitor strategy performance and correlations
3. Rebalance portfolio based on market conditions
4. Manage strategy lifecycle from inception to retirement

### Risk-Return Optimization
1. Balance risk and return across the portfolio
2. Implement dynamic position sizing
3. Monitor Sharpe ratios and risk-adjusted metrics
4. Adjust strategies for changing market regimes

## Composition
- **Chair:** Chief Investment Officer (CIO)
- **Members:** Portfolio managers, quantitative researchers, risk analysts
- **Term:** 2 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Weekly, with ad-hoc strategy sessions

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval for strategy changes
- **Urgent:** Chair authority for time-sensitive adjustments
- **Appeals:** Appeal to AAC Board

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Weekly performance reports
- **Content:** Strategy performance, new opportunities, risk metrics
- **Escalation:** Immediate reporting of significant strategy underperformance

## Resources
- Access to global market data and research
- Proprietary arbitrage modeling tools
- AI-driven strategy optimization platforms
- Historical arbitrage database (1000+ systems)

## Success Metrics
- Consistent outperformance of arbitrage benchmarks
- Successful deployment of new strategies
- Positive risk-adjusted returns across all strategies
- Continuous improvement in strategy efficiency

## Integration
- Coordinate with Risk Management Committee on strategy risks
- Collaborate with Technology Committee on execution systems
- Work with Compliance Committee on regulatory strategies
- Partner with Performance Committee on target achievement
