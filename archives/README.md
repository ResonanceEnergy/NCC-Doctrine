# Archives Directory

## Purpose
Historical records, completed projects, legacy documentation, and version-controlled archives for the NCC enterprise.

## Ownership
- **Primary Owner**: NCC Archives and Records Management
- **Secondary Owner**: Master Brain Storage Team
- **Maintenance Lead**: Archives Administration Team

## Directory Structure
- `/archives/` - Main archives directory
  - `versions/` - Version-controlled backups and historical versions
  - `backups/` - System and data backups
  - `completed-projects/` - Archived completed projects
  - `legacy/` - Legacy systems and deprecated documentation
  - `exports/` - Data exports and project exports

## Retention Policies
- **Operational Records**: 7 years minimum retention
- **Financial Records**: 10 years minimum retention
- **Legal Documents**: Permanent retention
- **Project Archives**: 5 years after project completion
- **System Backups**: 30 days rolling retention

## Maintenance Procedures
- **Archive Indexing**: Quarterly archive inventory and indexing
- **Data Integrity**: Monthly integrity checks on archived data
- **Access Logging**: All archive access logged and audited
- **Migration**: Legacy data migrated to current formats annually
- **Disposal**: Records disposed of according to retention schedules

## Access Control
- **Read Access**: Authorized personnel with business need
- **Write Access**: Archives administration team only
- **Execute Access**: Restricted to archive management operations
