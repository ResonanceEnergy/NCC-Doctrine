## Decision Engine (Digital vs Real-World Weighing)
### NCC Decision Template
- **Decision ID:** NCC-D-YYYY-###
- **Context:** Why it matters
- **Options:** A/B/C with pros/cons
- **Digital Advantage Score (0-10):**
  - Speed, Cost, Scalability, Auditability, Automation potential, Portability, Privacy/security, Dependency risk
- **Real-World Advantage Score (0-10):**
  - Trust/relationship impact, Physical quality constraints, Legal requirements, Emotional/values alignment, Irreplaceable experience, Risk appetite
- **Risk Register:** Top 3 risks + mitigations
- **Verdict:** Commit, defer, or experiment
- **Next Actions:** Owner, deadline, success metric

### Decision Rules
- Major decisions (money, identity, health, reputation) require Decision ID and memo
- Reversible decisions: Decide fast, log it
- Irreversible decisions: Demand evidence + redundancy
