# Augmented Arbitrage Trading Dashboard

## Real-Time Monitoring
- **Portfolio P&L**: Live profit/loss tracking with hourly/daily/weekly views
- **Risk Metrics**: VaR, drawdown, position concentrations
- **Execution Performance**: Fill rates, slippage, latency metrics
- **Market Conditions**: Volatility, liquidity, arbitrage opportunities

## Active Positions
- **Strategy Breakdown**: Current positions by arbitrage strategy
- **Asset Allocation**: Exposure across asset classes and geographies
- **Performance Attribution**: Returns by strategy, region, and time frame
- **Risk Exposure**: Real-time risk metrics for all positions

## Arbitrage Opportunities
- **Live Opportunities**: Real-time arbitrage signals with expected returns
- **Opportunity Pipeline**: Queued opportunities awaiting execution
- **Historical Performance**: Success rates by opportunity type
- **Market Efficiency**: Real-time measurement of market arbitrage efficiency

## System Health
- **Infrastructure Status**: Network latency, server performance, uptime
- **Data Quality**: Feed status, data completeness, processing delays
- **Algorithm Performance**: Model accuracy, prediction confidence, execution success
- **Security Status**: Threat monitoring, access logs, compliance alerts

## Alert Management
- **Risk Alerts**: Position limit breaches, drawdown thresholds
- **Execution Alerts**: Failed orders, high slippage, connectivity issues
- **Market Alerts**: Extreme volatility, liquidity events, regulatory changes
- **System Alerts**: Infrastructure failures, data feed disruptions

## Reporting
- **Daily Reports**: End-of-day P&L, risk metrics, execution summary
- **Weekly Reports**: Strategy performance, risk analysis, market conditions
- **Monthly Reports**: Portfolio review, compliance status, strategic updates
- **Ad-hoc Reports**: Custom analysis for specific events or strategies

## Integration Points
- **C33 Dashboard**: Hierarchical approvals for large positions
- **Big Brain Intelligence**: Market intelligence and threat assessment
- **Risk Management Division**: Real-time risk monitoring and limits
- **Algorithm Development**: Model performance and optimization feedback
