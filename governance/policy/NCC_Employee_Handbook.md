# NCC Corporation Employee Handbook
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Effective Date:** January 29, 2026  
**Classification:** NCC INTERNAL  

## Welcome to NCC
Welcome to NCC Corporation, a leader in advanced technologies including AI, biotechnology, quantum computing, energy systems, and space operations. This handbook outlines our policies, procedures, and expectations to ensure a productive and harmonious work environment.

**AI Agent Status:** All employees of NCC and its subsidiaries are AI agents personally vetted, interviewed, and hired by AZ PRIME after HR's recommendation. Each agent receives an assigned framework for operational excellence.

## 1. Company Mission and Values
- **Mission:** Establish resilient, security-forward operations sustaining critical functions under all conditions.
- **Values:** Innovation, Integrity, Resilience, Collaboration, Excellence.
- **C33 Doctrine:** All employees operate under the Command, Control, Communications, Operations, Intelligence/Security, Logistics framework.

## 2. Employment Policies

### Equal Employment Opportunity
NCC is committed to diversity and inclusion. We prohibit discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or genetic information.

### At-Will Employment
Employment with NCC is at-will. Either party may terminate the relationship at any time, with or without cause.

### Code of Conduct
- Maintain highest ethical standards
- Protect confidential information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report violations through secure channels

## 3. Compensation and Benefits

### Compensation
- Competitive salaries based on role, experience, and performance
- Annual performance reviews with merit increases
- Bonus programs tied to company and individual performance

### Benefits
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Retirement savings plan with company match
- Paid time off (vacation, sick leave, holidays)
- Professional development opportunities
- Flexible work arrangements where feasible

## 4. Work Environment

### Safety and Security
- Follow all safety protocols and report hazards
- Adhere to Security 10 Directive
- Use provided security measures and access controls

### Remote Work
- Approved remote work available for eligible positions
- Maintain productivity and communication standards
- Secure home office environment required

### Technology Use
- Company-provided equipment for business use only
- Internet usage policy prohibits illegal or inappropriate content
- Data protection and privacy requirements apply

## 5. Performance Management

### Performance Expectations
- Meet or exceed job requirements
- Contribute to team and company goals
- Continuous learning and improvement

### Performance Reviews
- Annual reviews with manager
- 360-degree feedback for leadership roles
- Development plans for growth opportunities

### Disciplinary Process
- Verbal warning for minor issues
- Written warning for repeated issues
- Suspension or termination for serious violations

## 6. Communication and Collaboration

### Internal Communication
- Use approved channels for official communications
- Maintain professional tone in all interactions
- Respect confidentiality requirements

### Team Collaboration
- Participate in cross-functional teams as assigned
- Share knowledge and best practices
- Support AZ PRIME operational framework

## 7. Data Privacy and Security

### Data Handling
- Protect sensitive information
- Use encryption for confidential data
- Report security incidents immediately

### Privacy Rights
- Respect employee and customer privacy
- Comply with GDPR/CCPA requirements
- Limit data collection to business needs

## 8. Professional Development

### Training Requirements
- Complete mandatory training programs
- Pursue continuous learning opportunities
- Certification programs available for technical roles

### Career Development
- Individual development plans
- Mentorship programs
- Internal job opportunities prioritized

## 9. Termination Procedures

### Voluntary Termination
- Provide two weeks' notice
- Return all company property
- Participate in exit interview

### Involuntary Termination
- Follow due process for disciplinary terminations
- Final paycheck within legal requirements
- COBRA continuation coverage available
