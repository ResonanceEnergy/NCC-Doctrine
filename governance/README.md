# Governance Directory

## Purpose
Board governance, compliance, risk management, policies, and strategic oversight for the NCC enterprise.

## Ownership
- **Primary Owner**: NCC Board of Directors
- **Secondary Owner**: C-Suite Executive Committee
- **Maintenance Lead**: Governance Compliance Team

## Directory Structure
- `/governance/` - Main governance directory
  - `board/` - Board meeting transcripts, minutes, and executive summaries
  - `strategic-planning/` - Long-term strategic plans and business development
  - `audit/` - Internal and external audit reports and procedures
  - `compliance/` - Regulatory compliance and legal frameworks
  - `risk/` - Risk assessment and management protocols
  - `legal/` - Legal documents, contracts, and frameworks

## Maintenance Procedures
- **Board Meetings**: Quarterly board meetings with documented minutes and action items
- **Compliance Reviews**: Annual compliance audits and regulatory reporting
- **Risk Assessments**: Quarterly enterprise risk assessments
- **Policy Updates**: Policies reviewed and updated annually or as needed
- **Audit Cycles**: Continuous audit monitoring with annual comprehensive audits
