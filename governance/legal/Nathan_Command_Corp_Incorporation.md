# OPERATING AGREEMENT
# Nathan Command Corp LLC

## 1. FORMATION
1.1 Formation. Nathan Command Corp LLC (the "Company") is formed as a Delaware limited liability company pursuant to the Delaware Limited Liability Company Act (6 Del. C. ยง 18-101 et seq.) and this Operating Agreement.

1.5 Term. The term of the Company shall be perpetual unless dissolved pursuant to Article X.

## 2. PURPOSE
The Company shall engage in the following business activities:
- Holding and managing investments worldwide
- Operating and managing subsidiaries and affiliates
- Providing strategic management and consulting services
- Developing and deploying advanced technologies
- Conducting research and development activities
- Any other lawful business activities

## 3. MANAGEMENT
3.1 Manager-Managed. The Company shall be manager-managed. Management authority is vested in the Board of Managers.

3.2 Board of Managers. The Board shall consist of 5-7 Managers appointed by the Members.

3.3 Powers. The Board shall have full authority to manage the Company's business and affairs.

3.4 AZ PRIME Integration. The Company shall utilize AZ PRIME AI systems for operational decision-making and strategic guidance.

## 4. CAPITAL AND OWNERSHIP
4.1 Capital Contributions. Initial capital contribution: $100,000.00

4.2 Ownership. 100% ownership by founding member.

4.3 Additional Contributions. Additional capital may be contributed as determined by the Board.

## 5. DISTRIBUTIONS
5.1 Distributions. Distributions shall be made at the discretion of the Board of Managers.

5.2 Tax Treatment. The Company shall be treated as a partnership for U.S. federal income tax purposes.

## 6. BOOKS AND RECORDS
6.1 Maintenance. The Company shall maintain complete and accurate books and records.

6.2 Inspection. Members shall have the right to inspect Company records.

## 7. TRANSFERS
7.1 Restrictions. Transfers of membership interests are restricted.

7.2 Right of First Offer. The Company shall have a right of first offer on any proposed transfer.

## 8. DISSOLUTION
8.1 Events of Dissolution. The Company may be dissolved by majority vote of the Members or by operation of law.

8.2 Liquidation. Upon dissolution, assets shall be distributed according to capital account balances.

## 9. INDEMNIFICATION
9.1 Indemnification. The Company shall indemnify Managers and Members for actions taken in good faith.

## 10. MISCELLANEOUS
10.1 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by Delaware law.

10.2 Amendments. This Agreement may be amended by majority vote of the Members.

10.3 Severability. If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in effect.
