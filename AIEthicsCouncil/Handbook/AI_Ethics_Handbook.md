# AI Ethics Council Handbook
**Operational Guidelines and Procedures**

## Overview
This handbook provides operational procedures for the AI Ethics Council, ensuring consistent application of ethical standards across all NCC AI development and deployment activities.

## Daily Operations

### Ethics Review Process
1. **Project Submission:** All AI projects require ethics review form
2. **Initial Assessment:** 24-hour preliminary review for compliance
3. **Full Review:** Comprehensive assessment within 7 business days
4. **Approval/Recommendations:** Final determination with conditions if needed

### Monitoring and Auditing
- Monthly compliance audits for active AI systems
- Quarterly risk assessments for high-impact projects
- Annual comprehensive ethics review for all AI operations

## Emergency Procedures

### AI System Failures
1. Immediate containment and isolation
2. Ethics council emergency notification
3. Root cause analysis and corrective actions
4. Incident reporting and documentation

### Ethical Violations
1. Immediate suspension of non-compliant activities
2. Internal investigation and fact-finding
3. Corrective action plan development
4. Preventive measures implementation

## Training Requirements

### Mandatory Training
- Annual ethics training for all AI developers
- Project-specific ethics briefings
- Security awareness and data privacy training

### Certification Programs
- AI Ethics Specialist certification
- Ethics Review Board member training
- Compliance officer qualification program

## Communication Protocols

### Internal Communications
- Daily ethics briefing reports
- Weekly council meetings
- Monthly division-wide updates

### External Communications
- Transparent reporting of ethics initiatives
- Stakeholder engagement on AI policies
- Public disclosure of significant findings

## Quality Assurance

### Standards Compliance
- Regular audits against international AI ethics standards
- Continuous improvement of ethical frameworks
- Benchmarking against industry best practices

### Performance Metrics
- Ethics violation rates
- Review process efficiency
- Stakeholder satisfaction scores
- Compliance achievement rates
