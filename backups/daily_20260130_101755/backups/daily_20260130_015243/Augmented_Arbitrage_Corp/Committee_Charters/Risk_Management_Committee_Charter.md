# Risk Management Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The Risk Management Committee (RMC) is responsible for overseeing all risk aspects of Augmented Arbitrage Corp's operations, ensuring the preservation of capital while maximizing arbitrage returns.

## Mission
To establish and maintain comprehensive risk management frameworks that protect AAC's capital while enabling the pursuit of arbitrage opportunities with minimal downside risk.

## Authority
- **Risk Limits:** Final authority on position and portfolio risk limits
- **Emergency Actions:** Authority to halt trading and liquidate positions
- **Risk Models:** Approval of risk measurement and monitoring systems
- **Stress Testing:** Oversight of scenario analysis and stress testing

## Responsibilities
### Risk Assessment
1. Identify and quantify all arbitrage-related risks
2. Monitor position-level and portfolio-level risk metrics
3. Assess counterparty and liquidity risks
4. Evaluate model and execution risks

### Risk Controls
1. Establish and enforce risk limits across all strategies
2. Implement automated risk monitoring systems
3. Develop contingency plans for adverse scenarios
4. Conduct regular risk audits and reviews

### Crisis Management
1. Monitor for early warning signals of risk events
2. Execute emergency protocols when risk thresholds breached
3. Coordinate with external parties during crises
4. Conduct post-crisis analysis and improvement

## Composition
- **Chair:** Chief Risk Officer (CRO)
- **Members:** Risk analysts, quantitative risk managers, operations specialists
- **Term:** 2 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Daily risk review, weekly full committee

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval for limit changes
- **Emergency:** Chair unilateral authority for immediate actions
- **Appeals:** Appeal to AAC Board

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Daily risk reports, weekly comprehensive reviews
- **Content:** Risk exposures, limit breaches, mitigation actions
- **Escalation:** Immediate notification of critical risk events

## Resources
- Real-time risk monitoring systems
- Historical risk scenario databases
- Advanced risk modeling software
- Emergency response protocols and teams

## Success Metrics
- Zero breaches of risk limits
- Minimal losses from risk events
- Comprehensive risk coverage across all operations
- Continuous improvement in risk management capabilities

## Integration
- Coordinate with Arbitrage Strategy Committee on risk-return tradeoffs
- Collaborate with Technology Committee on risk system development
- Work with Compliance Committee on regulatory risk management
- Partner with Performance Committee on risk-adjusted metrics
