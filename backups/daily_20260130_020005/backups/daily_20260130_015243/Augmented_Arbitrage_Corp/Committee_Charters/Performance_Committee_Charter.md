# Performance Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The Performance Committee (PC) oversees the performance measurement, target setting, and compensation systems for Augmented Arbitrage Corp.

## Mission
To establish and monitor performance metrics that drive AAC toward consistent, superior risk-adjusted returns while aligning compensation with long-term value creation.

## Authority
- **Performance Targets:** Final authority on performance objectives and benchmarks
- **Compensation Structure:** Approval of compensation and incentive plans
- **Performance Evaluation:** Oversight of performance measurement and attribution
- **Resource Allocation:** Control over performance-based capital allocation

## Responsibilities
### Performance Measurement
1. Develop comprehensive performance metrics and benchmarks
2. Monitor risk-adjusted returns and Sharpe ratios
3. Conduct performance attribution analysis
4. Track progress toward AUM and growth targets

### Target Setting
1. Establish realistic yet ambitious performance targets
2. Adjust targets based on market conditions and opportunities
3. Balance short-term performance with long-term sustainability
4. Communicate performance expectations across the organization

### Compensation Management
1. Design performance-based compensation structures
2. Monitor incentive alignment with risk management
3. Ensure competitive compensation for top talent
4. Review and adjust compensation plans annually

## Composition
- **Chair:** Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- **Members:** Executives, portfolio managers, compensation specialists
- **Term:** 2 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Monthly, with quarterly compensation reviews

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval for target and compensation changes
- **Strategic:** Chair authority for major performance decisions
- **Appeals:** Appeal to AAC Board

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Monthly performance reports
- **Content:** Performance metrics, target progress, compensation analysis
- **Escalation:** Immediate reporting of significant performance deviations

## Resources
- Performance measurement and attribution systems
- Compensation benchmarking data
- External performance consultants
- Historical performance databases

## Success Metrics
- Achievement of performance targets consistently
- Superior risk-adjusted returns vs. benchmarks
- Effective incentive alignment
- Attraction and retention of top talent

## Integration
- Coordinate with Arbitrage Strategy Committee on performance drivers
- Collaborate with Risk Management Committee on risk-adjusted metrics
- Work with Technology Committee on performance systems
- Partner with Compliance Committee on regulatory performance requirements
