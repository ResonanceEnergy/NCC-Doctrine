# Advanced Materials Corporation Handbook
**Operational Guidelines and Procedures**

## Overview
This handbook provides operational procedures for Advanced Materials Corporation, ensuring safe and efficient materials research, development, and production.

## Daily Operations

### Laboratory Safety
1. **Personal Protective Equipment (PPE):** Mandatory for all lab access
2. **Hazard Communication:** Proper labeling and documentation
3. **Emergency Equipment:** Location and operation verification
4. **Waste Management:** Proper disposal procedures

### Research Operations
- Experimental planning and approval
- Equipment calibration and maintenance
- Data collection and documentation
- Quality control procedures

## Emergency Procedures

### Chemical Spills
1. Evacuation of affected area
2. Spill containment and cleanup
3. Medical attention if needed
4. Incident reporting and investigation

### Equipment Failures
1. Immediate shutdown and isolation
2. Safety assessment and hazard evaluation
3. Repair or replacement coordination
4. Process documentation and review
