# AI Governance Council Doctrine
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATRIX COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Executive Summary
The AI Governance Council (AIGC) serves as the supreme authority for all artificial intelligence development, deployment, and oversight within NATRIX COMMAND CORP. As an independent council reporting directly to Real World Nate, AIGC ensures that all AI systems align with NCC's mission of ethical, profitable, and sustainable growth through 2100 and beyond.

## Core Mission
- Establish ethical frameworks for AI development and deployment
- Oversee AI risk management across all NCC entities
- Ensure AI systems enhance human potential without compromising security
- Drive innovation in AI technologies for competitive advantage
- Maintain compliance with evolving AI regulations and standards

## Governance Structure
### Leadership
- **Council Chair:** Appointed by Real World Nate
- **Executive Director:** Full-time AI governance expert
- **Committee Chairs:** Five specialized committees
- **Technical Advisors:** Leading AI researchers and ethicists

### Reporting Lines
- Direct reporting to Real World Nate
- Advisory role to all NCC company CEOs
- Coordination with AZ PRIME for operational integration
- Collaboration with Ludwig Law Corp for legal compliance

## AI Development Principles
### Ethical AI Framework
1. **Human-Centric Design:** All AI systems must enhance human capabilities and decision-making
2. **Transparency:** Clear explainability of AI decision processes
3. **Fairness:** Elimination of bias in AI algorithms and outcomes
4. **Accountability:** Clear responsibility chains for AI actions
5. **Safety:** Robust safeguards against unintended consequences

### Risk Management
- Continuous monitoring of AI system performance
- Regular audits of AI decision-making processes
- Emergency protocols for AI system failures
- Ethical review boards for high-risk AI applications

### AI Strategy Committee (ASC)
