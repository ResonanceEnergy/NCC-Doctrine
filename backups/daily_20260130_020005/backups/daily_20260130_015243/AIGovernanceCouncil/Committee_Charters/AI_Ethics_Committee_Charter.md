# AI Ethics Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The AI Ethics Committee (AEC) is responsible for establishing and maintaining ethical standards for all AI development and deployment within NATHAN COMMAND CORP.

## Mission
To ensure all AI systems developed, deployed, or operated by NCC adhere to the highest ethical standards, prioritizing human welfare, fairness, transparency, and accountability.

## Authority
- **Ethical Approval:** Final authority on ethical compliance for all AI projects
- **Guidelines Development:** Create and update AI ethical frameworks
- **Project Review:** Mandatory review of all AI initiatives
- **Veto Power:** Halt unethical AI projects

## Responsibilities
### Ethical Framework Development
1. Develop comprehensive AI ethics guidelines
2. Monitor emerging ethical challenges in AI
3. Update standards based on technological advancements
4. Provide ethical training to NCC personnel

### Project Oversight
1. Review all AI project proposals for ethical compliance
2. Conduct ethical impact assessments
3. Monitor ongoing AI projects for ethical adherence
4. Investigate ethical concerns or violations

### Stakeholder Engagement
1. Collaborate with external ethicists and AI experts
2. Engage with regulatory bodies on ethical standards
3. Educate NCC leadership on AI ethics
4. Foster industry dialogue on responsible AI

## Composition
- **Chair:** Appointed by AIGC Council Chair
- **Members:** 5-7 ethics experts, philosophers, and AI specialists
- **Term:** 3 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Bi-weekly, with emergency sessions as needed

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval required
- **Appeals:** Appeal to full AIGC Council

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Monthly reports to AIGC Council
- **Content:** Ethical compliance status, emerging issues, recommendations
- **Escalation:** Immediate reporting of critical ethical concerns

## Resources
- Dedicated budget for ethical research and training
- Access to external ethical consultants
- Independent audit authority for ethical compliance

## Success Metrics
- Zero ethical violations in AI systems
- 100% ethical review completion for AI projects
- Positive industry recognition for ethical AI leadership
- Continuous improvement in ethical standards

## Integration
- Coordinate with AI Risk Management Committee on ethical risk assessment
- Collaborate with AI Compliance Committee on regulatory ethics
- Work with AI Development Committee on ethical R&D practices
