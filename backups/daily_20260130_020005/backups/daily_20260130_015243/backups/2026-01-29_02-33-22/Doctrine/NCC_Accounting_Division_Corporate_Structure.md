## Executive Summary
The NCC Accounting Division has been restructured following the organizational models of the world's top 25 international accounting firms (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG, and their global competitors). This structure emphasizes:

- **Global Network Architecture** with regional hubs
- **Service Line Specialization** across audit, tax, advisory, and consulting
- **Industry Sector Expertise** with dedicated vertical teams
- **Technology Integration** with AI-driven analytics
- **Regulatory Compliance** across 50+ jurisdictions
- **Client Relationship Management** with enterprise-level service delivery
