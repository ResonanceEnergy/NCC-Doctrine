# ADMIN Division Doctrine
**Version:** 1.0
**Classification:** NCC CONFIDENTIAL
**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026

## Mission Statement
The ADMIN Division provides comprehensive administrative support to NCC operations, including human resources, accounting, and global talent acquisition, ensuring operational excellence and agent development.

## Core Principles
1. **Agent Excellence**: Continuous development and evolution of NCC talent
2. **Operational Support**: Seamless administrative services
3. **Compliance Assurance**: Regulatory and policy adherence
4. **Strategic HR**: Proactive talent management and acquisition

### Accounting & Finance

### Global Talent Acquisition
