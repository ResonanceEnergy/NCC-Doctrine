# Operations Directory

## Purpose
Enterprise-wide operational procedures, frameworks, and protocols for NCC continuous operations and business processes.

## Ownership
- **Primary Owner**: NCC Operations Command Center
- **Secondary Owner**: AZ PRIME Executive Operations
- **Maintenance Lead**: Operations Quality Assurance Team

## Directory Structure
- `/operations/` - Main operations directory
  - `continuous-operations/` - 24/7 operations frameworks and procedures
  - `agent-deployment/` - Agent hiring, training, and deployment protocols
  - `monitoring/` - System monitoring, metrics, and reporting
  - `deployment/` - Software and system deployment procedures
  - `incident-response/` - Incident response and crisis management
  - `quality-assurance/` - QA testing and validation procedures

## Maintenance Procedures
- **Cycle Updates**: Operations procedures updated quarterly with cycle-based auditing
- **Performance Metrics**: Monthly review of operational KPIs and efficiency metrics
- **Training**: Annual mandatory training for all operations personnel
- **Audit**: Quarterly internal audits by Operations Quality Assurance
- **Documentation**: All procedures must be documented with clear ownership and review cycles
