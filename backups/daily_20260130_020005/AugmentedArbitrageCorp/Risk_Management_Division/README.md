# Risk Management Division

## Overview
The Risk Management Division implements position sizing, drawdown protection, and portfolio optimization for Augmented Arbitrage Corp. We ensure that arbitrage strategies maintain acceptable risk-adjusted returns while protecting capital during adverse market conditions.

## Mission
To optimize risk-adjusted returns through dynamic position sizing, correlation analysis, and comprehensive risk monitoring across all arbitrage strategies.

## Key Products
- **Dynamic Risk Allocation System**: Real-time position sizing based on volatility and correlation
- **Portfolio Stress Testing Framework**: Scenario analysis for extreme market conditions
- **Drawdown Protection Mechanisms**: Automatic position reduction during losses

## Team Structure
- **Director of Risk Management**: Overall risk strategy and policy
- **Chief Risk Officer**: Enterprise risk oversight
- **Quantitative Risk Analyst**: Model development and validation
- **Portfolio Risk Manager**: Position-level risk monitoring
- **Compliance Officer**: Regulatory risk management

## Risk Framework
- **Value at Risk (VaR)**: 99% confidence, 1-day horizon
- **Expected Shortfall (ES)**: Tail risk measurement
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Portfolio-level loss limits
- **Stress Testing**: Historical and hypothetical scenarios

## Position Sizing Methodology
- **Kelly Criterion**: Optimal position sizing for positive expected value trades
- **Risk Parity**: Equal risk contribution across strategies
- **Volatility Targeting**: Consistent portfolio volatility regardless of market conditions
- **Correlation Adjustment**: Reduced sizing for correlated positions

## Risk Limits
- **Portfolio VaR**: <2% daily loss limit
- **Strategy VaR**: <0.5% per individual strategy
- **Drawdown Limits**: 5% maximum portfolio drawdown before reduction
- **Concentration Limits**: <10% in any single arbitrage strategy

## Monitoring & Controls
- **Real-time Risk Dashboard**: Live position and risk metrics
- **Automated Alerts**: Breach notifications and automatic responses
- **Daily Risk Reports**: End-of-day risk assessment and attribution
- **Monthly Risk Reviews**: Strategy-level risk analysis and adjustments

## Regulatory Compliance
- **Dodd-Frank**: Position limits and reporting requirements
- **MiFID II**: Transaction reporting and best execution
- **SEC Regulations**: Market manipulation prevention
- **CFTC Oversight**: Derivatives position reporting

## Integration Points
- **Algorithm Development**: Risk constraints for strategy development
- **Market Intelligence**: Volatility data for position sizing
- **Execution Infrastructure**: Risk checks in order flow
