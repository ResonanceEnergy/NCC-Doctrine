## Community Review Process (Feb 22-28)

1. **List shared with team**
   - What's being considered for deletion
   - Why (it's a duplicate)
   - When (after Mar 1 approval)

2. **Team reviews**
   - "Keep this, delete that"
   - Explain reasoning
   - Highlight institutional knowledge

3. **Consolidate feedback**
   - Identify consensus items
   - Flag controversial items
   - Create final deletion list

4. **Document decisions**
   - Why each item approved/rejected
   - Who approved it
   - Reference for future
