## SECTION 1: WORLDWIDE APPLICATIONS & USE CASES

### **A. PRIMARY APPLICATIONS (Revenue-Generating)**

**1. Rural Off-Grid Electrification**
- **Power Range:** 5–20 kW per household cluster
- **Load Profile:** Evening peak (lighting, cooking, entertainment); productive use (irrigation pumps, milling)
- **Business Model:** EaaS/paygo ($0.08–0.15/kWh); community cooperative ownership
- **Target Markets:** Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, Southeast Asia, Latin America, remote North America/Australia
- **Addressable:** 800M+ people lacking grid access

**2. Agricultural Irrigation + Power**
- **Power Range:** 10–50 kW (irrigation pump + farm loads)
- **Load Profile:** Daytime irrigation; household/workshop loads year-round
- **Business Model:** Farmer cooperatives; MFI financing; avoided diesel cost (~$0.40/kWh)
- **Target Markets:** India, Nepal, Sub-Saharan Africa, Central Asia, Andes region
- **Addressable:** 500M+ smallholder farmers with water access

**3. Community Microgrids**
- **Power Range:** 20–200 kW (multi-unit clusters + storage)
- **Load Profile:** Residential + commercial (shops, clinics, schools); 24/7 baseload
- **Business Model:** SPV/cooperative; grant/DFI co-finance; prepaid tariffs
- **Target Markets:** Island nations, remote villages globally
- **Addressable:** 10,000+ communities (50–500 households each)

**4. Agro-Processing & SMEs**
- **Power Range:** 20–100 kW (grain mills, cold storage, sawmills, workshops)
- **Load Profile:** Daytime productive use; high load factor
- **Business Model:** Direct PPA with SME; 5–10 year contracts; clear ROI (<3 yr payback)
- **Target Markets:** All developing regions with ag/forestry value chains
- **Addressable:** 100,000+ SMEs globally

**5. Eco-Lodges & Remote Facilities**
- **Power Range:** 5–30 kW (lodges, research stations, mining camps)
- **Load Profile:** 24/7 with peaks for guest services
- **Business Model:** Lease or direct purchase; premium for green branding; diesel displacement
- **Target Markets:** Ecotourism destinations, protected areas, resource extraction sites
- **Addressable:** 10,000+ facilities globally

**6. Municipal/Utility Co-Generation**
- **Power Range:** 50–500 kW (WWTP/WTP, canal controls, reservoir spillways)
- **Load Profile:** Steady baseload matching facility operations
- **Business Model:** Utility ownership or IPP/PPA; net metering; revenue share
- **Target Markets:** North America, Europe, developed Asia, progressive developing-country utilities
- **Addressable:** 50,000+ municipal water facilities globally

**7. Telecom/Critical Infrastructure**
- **Power Range:** 5–20 kW (cell towers, repeaters, clinics, emergency services)
- **Load Profile:** 24/7 critical; high reliability requirement (>99%)
- **Business Model:** ESCO/PPA with telecom operator; avoided diesel/battery cost
- **Target Markets:** Areas with mobile coverage gaps, rural health systems
- **Addressable:** 100,000+ sites globally

---

### **B. EMERGING/FUTURE APPLICATIONS**

**8. Hydrogen Production (Small-Scale)**
- **Power Range:** 50–500 kW for electrolyzer
- **Use Case:** Green hydrogen for ag (fertilizer), transport (fuel cells), energy storage
- **Timeline:** Year 5+; depends on electrolyzer cost-down and H₂ offtake
- **Markets:** High renewable-penetration grids; ag regions with water+power

**9. Disaster Relief & Humanitarian**
- **Power Range:** 5–20 kW portable/modular units
- **Use Case:** Rapid deployment post-disaster; refugee camps; field hospitals
- **Business Model:** NGO procurement; government emergency stockpiles
- **Markets:** Disaster-prone regions; humanitarian agencies

**10. Industrial Campus Distributed Generation**
- **Power Range:** 100–1000 kW (multi-unit clusters)
- **Use Case:** Food processing, textiles, light manufacturing with on-site water
- **Business Model:** Behind-the-meter PPA; demand charge reduction; resilience
- **Markets:** South Asia, Southeast Asia, Latin America industrial zones

**11. Virtual Power Plants (VPP) & Grid Services**
- **Power Range:** Aggregated fleets (MW-scale equivalent)
- **Use Case:** Frequency regulation, peak shaving, demand response
- **Business Model:** Revenue stacking (energy + capacity + ancillary services)
- **Markets:** Liberalized electricity markets (US, EU, Australia, parts of Asia)
- **Timeline:** Year 3–5; regulatory enablement required
