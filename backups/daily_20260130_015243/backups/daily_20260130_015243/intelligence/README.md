# Intelligence Directory

## Purpose
Enterprise intelligence gathering, analysis, market research, and strategic insights across all NCC operations.

## Ownership
- **Primary Owner**: NCC Intelligence Command Center
- **Secondary Owner**: NCL Insights Division
- **Maintenance Lead**: Intelligence Analysis Team

## Directory Structure
- `/intelligence/` - Main intelligence directory
  - `market/` - Market intelligence and competitive analysis
  - `operational/` - Operational intelligence and threat assessment
  - `strategic/` - Strategic intelligence and long-term forecasting
  - `threat/` - Cybersecurity and threat intelligence
  - `ncl-insights/` - NCL insights distribution and integration
  - `master-brain/` - Master Brain storage analysis and insights

## Maintenance Procedures
- **Intelligence Cycles**: Weekly intelligence briefings and monthly comprehensive reports
- **Data Validation**: All intelligence data validated through multiple sources
- **Security Classification**: Intelligence properly classified and access-controlled
- **Archive Management**: Historical intelligence archived with retention policies
- **Quality Assurance**: Intelligence products reviewed by senior analysts
