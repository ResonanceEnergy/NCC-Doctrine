# Tactical Support Ops Packet (TS)
**Purpose:** Quick brief pack for operations.  
**Format:** SMEAC (5-paragraph order)

## Slide 1 – Situation
- Area, weather, terrain, civil
- Friendly/hostile/unknown actors

## Slide 2 – Mission
- Who, What, Where, When, Why (5W)

## Slide 3 – Execution
- Concept of operations
- Tasks to sub-units
- Coordinating instructions

## Slide 4 – Sustainment
- Supply, transport, medical, maintenance

## Slide 5 – Signals
- Primary/Alt comms, call signs, brevity, time sync

---
**Owner:** S3 | **Update Cycle:** Per operation
