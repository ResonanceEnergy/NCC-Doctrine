# Technology Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The Technology Committee (TC) oversees all technological aspects of Augmented Arbitrage Corp, ensuring cutting-edge systems for arbitrage execution and AI augmentation.

## Mission
To develop and maintain world-class technology infrastructure that enables AAC to execute arbitrage strategies with maximum speed, efficiency, and competitive advantage.

## Authority
- **Technology Roadmap:** Final authority on technology strategy and investments
- **System Development:** Approval for new system development and procurement
- **Infrastructure Management:** Control over IT infrastructure and security
- **Innovation Funding:** Allocation of technology R&D budget

## Responsibilities
### System Development
1. Oversee development of arbitrage execution systems
2. Implement AI and machine learning capabilities
3. Maintain high-frequency trading infrastructure
4. Develop risk management and monitoring systems

### Infrastructure Management
1. Manage data centers and trading infrastructure
2. Ensure system reliability and uptime
3. Implement cybersecurity measures
4. Optimize for low-latency execution

### Innovation and Research
1. Research emerging technologies for arbitrage
2. Develop proprietary algorithms and models
3. Explore quantum computing applications
4. Maintain technological competitive advantage

## Composition
- **Chair:** Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- **Members:** Technology architects, software engineers, quant developers
- **Term:** 2 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Bi-weekly, with ad-hoc technology reviews

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval for technology decisions
- **Urgent:** Chair authority for critical system issues
- **Appeals:** Appeal to AAC Board

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly technology status reports
- **Content:** System performance, development progress, security status
- **Escalation:** Immediate reporting of system failures or breaches

## Resources
- Dedicated technology development budget
- Access to cutting-edge hardware and software
- Internal development teams and external partnerships
- Secure development and testing environments

## Success Metrics
- 99.99% system uptime and reliability
- Sub-millisecond execution speeds for HFT strategies
- Continuous technological innovation and improvement
- Superior system performance compared to competitors

## Integration
- Coordinate with Arbitrage Strategy Committee on algorithm development
- Collaborate with Risk Management Committee on risk systems
- Work with Compliance Committee on secure and compliant systems
- Partner with Performance Committee on technology-driven returns
