# Big Brain Intelligence Doctrine

## Mission Statement
Big Brain Intelligence (BBIC) serves as NCC's premier intelligence and data analysis division, providing comprehensive intelligence gathering, analysis, and strategic insights to support NCC operations and decision-making.

## Core Principles
1. **Information Superiority**: Maintain absolute dominance in intelligence gathering and analysis
2. **Security First**: All operations conducted under Security 10 Directive compliance
3. **Innovation Leadership**: Pioneer advanced AI and machine learning applications
4. **Cross-Departmental Collaboration**: Seamless integration with all NCC divisions
5. **Continuous Evolution**: Constant adaptation to emerging threats and opportunities
