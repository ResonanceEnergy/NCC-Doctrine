# AI Risk Management Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The AI Risk Management Committee (ARMC) is responsible for identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks associated with AI systems across NATHAN COMMAND CORP.

## Mission
To proactively manage AI-related risks, ensuring the safety, security, and stability of all AI systems while maximizing their benefits to NCC operations.

## Authority
- **Risk Assessment:** Comprehensive authority to evaluate AI risks
- **Mitigation Approval:** Final approval for risk mitigation strategies
- **Emergency Response:** Authority to initiate emergency protocols
- **Shutdown Power:** Emergency shutdown authority for high-risk AI systems

## Responsibilities
### Risk Assessment
1. Conduct regular risk assessments of all AI systems
2. Identify emerging AI risks and vulnerabilities
3. Evaluate third-party AI components and integrations
4. Monitor AI system performance and stability

### Risk Mitigation
1. Develop and implement risk mitigation strategies
2. Establish safety protocols and fail-safes
3. Coordinate incident response planning
4. Oversee AI system testing and validation

### Monitoring and Response
1. Continuous monitoring of AI system health
2. Rapid response to AI-related incidents
3. Post-incident analysis and improvement
4. Regular stress testing of AI systems

## Composition
- **Chair:** Appointed by AIGC Council Chair
- **Members:** 5-7 risk management, cybersecurity, and AI safety experts
- **Term:** 3 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Weekly, with 24/7 on-call availability

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval for standard decisions
- **Emergency:** Chair can make unilateral decisions in crises
- **Appeals:** Appeal to full AIGC Council

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Weekly reports to AIGC Council
- **Content:** Risk assessments, mitigation status, incident reports
- **Escalation:** Immediate reporting of critical risks or incidents

## Resources
- Access to all NCC AI systems and data
- Dedicated cybersecurity and risk analysis tools
- Emergency response team and protocols
- Independent budget for risk management activities

## Success Metrics
- Zero critical AI incidents or failures
- 100% risk assessment coverage of AI systems
- Rapid response times to identified risks
- Continuous improvement in AI system resilience

## Integration
- Coordinate with AI Ethics Committee on ethical risk assessment
- Collaborate with AI Compliance Committee on regulatory risks
- Work with AI Development Committee on risk-aware development practices
- Integrate with AZ PRIME for 24/7 monitoring
