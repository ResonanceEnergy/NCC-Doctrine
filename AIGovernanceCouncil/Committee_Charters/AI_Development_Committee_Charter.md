# AI Development Committee Charter
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Classification:** NATHAN COMMAND CORP TOP SECRET | **Date:** 2026-01-28

## Committee Overview
The AI Development Committee (ADC) oversees all AI research, development, and innovation activities within NATHAN COMMAND CORP.

## Mission
To drive cutting-edge AI innovation that provides competitive advantages to NCC while ensuring responsible development practices and maximum ROI.

## Authority
- **R&D Oversight:** Authority over all AI research and development
- **Technology Evaluation:** Final approval for AI technology adoption
- **Talent Management:** Oversight of AI talent acquisition and development
- **Innovation Funding:** Control over AI R&D budget allocation

## Responsibilities
### Research and Development
1. Oversee AI research initiatives across NCC
2. Evaluate emerging AI technologies for potential adoption
3. Coordinate AI development across different NCC entities
4. Monitor AI research trends and breakthroughs

### Technology Integration
1. Assess AI technologies for NCC applications
2. Develop integration strategies for new AI capabilities
3. Ensure compatibility with existing NCC systems
4. Manage AI technology procurement and licensing

### Talent and Capability Building
1. Develop AI talent acquisition strategies
2. Oversee AI training and education programs
3. Build partnerships with leading AI research institutions
4. Foster internal AI expertise development

## Composition
- **Chair:** Appointed by AIGC Council Chair
- **Members:** 5-7 AI researchers, engineers, and technology leaders
- **Term:** 3 years, renewable
- **Meetings:** Bi-weekly, with ad-hoc innovation sessions

## Decision-Making
- **Quorum:** 60% of members
- **Voting:** Majority approval for technology decisions
- **Innovation:** Fast-track approval for high-potential projects
- **Appeals:** Appeal to full AIGC Council

## Reporting
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly reports to AIGC Council
- **Content:** R&D progress, technology evaluations, innovation pipeline
- **Escalation:** Immediate reporting of breakthrough opportunities

## Resources
- Dedicated R&D budget ($200M annually)
- Access to NCC's AI infrastructure and data
- Partnerships with leading AI research institutions
- Internal AI development teams and labs

## Success Metrics
- Measurable ROI from AI innovations
- Industry leadership in AI technology adoption
- Successful deployment of AI solutions across NCC
- Continuous advancement of AI capabilities

## Integration
- Coordinate with AI Ethics Committee for ethical R&D practices
- Collaborate with AI Risk Management Committee on safe development
- Work with AI Compliance Committee on regulatory technology standards
- Partner with AI Strategy Committee for long-term technology roadmap
