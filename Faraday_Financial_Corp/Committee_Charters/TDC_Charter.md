# TECHNOLOGY & DATA COMMITTEE (TDC) CHARTER

**Faraday Financial Corporation LLC**  
**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]  
**Approved By:** Board of Managers  

## 1. PURPOSE AND AUTHORITY

The Technology & Data Committee (TDC) is established to oversee the design, implementation, and maintenance of the Company's technology infrastructure, data management systems, and cybersecurity framework. The TDC shall ensure that technology capabilities support trading operations, risk management, and regulatory compliance while maintaining operational resilience and data integrity.

## 2. COMPOSITION

### 2.1 Membership
- Chair: Chief Technology Officer
- Members: Chief Risk Officer, Head of Quantitative Research, infrastructure lead, and cybersecurity expert
- Secretary: Designated by the Chair

### 2.2 Term
Members serve at the pleasure of the Board of Managers.

### 2.3 Expertise
Committee members shall have expertise in technology infrastructure, data management, cybersecurity, and financial systems.

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES

### 3.1 Technology Infrastructure
- Oversee trading platform architecture and execution systems
- Monitor system performance and scalability requirements
- Plan technology upgrades and infrastructure investments
- Ensure business continuity and disaster recovery capabilities

### 3.2 Data Management
- Establish data governance and quality control procedures
- Monitor data pipeline integrity and processing accuracy
- Assess data storage, security, and retention policies
- Coordinate data acquisition and third-party data relationships

### 3.3 Cybersecurity
- Develop and maintain cybersecurity policies and procedures
- Monitor threat intelligence and vulnerability assessments
- Oversee incident response and breach containment protocols
- Ensure compliance with data privacy and security regulations

### 3.4 System Integration
- Coordinate integration between trading, risk, and research systems
- Monitor API connectivity and data flow reliability
- Assess vendor relationships and third-party system dependencies
- Plan system migrations and technology transitions

### 3.5 Innovation and Development
- Evaluate emerging technologies and their potential applications
- Oversee research and development of proprietary technology
- Monitor industry trends and competitive technology landscape
- Plan technology roadmap and capability enhancements

## 4. MEETINGS AND PROCEDURES

### 4.1 Regular Meetings
- Weekly technology operations and system health review
- Monthly technology strategy and planning sessions
- Quarterly cybersecurity and data governance assessment

### 4.2 Quorum and Voting
- Quorum: Majority of members
- Decisions: Majority vote for standard technology decisions, supermajority for major infrastructure changes

### 4.3 Decision Rights
- Authority to approve technology investments and system changes
- Power to implement emergency security measures
- Escalation to Board for strategic technology decisions or security incidents

### 4.4 Documentation
- All technology decisions and system changes documented
- Security incidents and response actions logged
- System performance and capacity planning tracked

## 5. REPORTING AND OVERSIGHT

### 5.1 Board Reporting
- Weekly system uptime and performance metrics
- Monthly technology status and project updates
- Quarterly cybersecurity assessment and technology roadmap

### 5.2 Metrics and KPIs
- System uptime: 99.9% availability target
- Data quality: >99.5% accuracy and completeness
- Security incidents: Zero material breaches
- Technology ROI: Positive return on technology investments

### 5.3 Annual Review
- Technology strategy effectiveness assessment
- Cybersecurity program review and updates
- Infrastructure capacity and scalability analysis

## 6. RESOURCES AND SUPPORT

### 6.1 Technology Team
- Dedicated infrastructure, development, and security teams
- Access to cloud providers and technology vendors
- Independent security assessment and audit capabilities

### 6.2 Technology
- Enterprise monitoring and management platforms
- Security information and event management (SIEM) systems
- Data quality and governance tools
- Development and testing environments

### 6.3 Training
- Technology operations and system administration training
- Cybersecurity awareness and incident response training
- Data governance and privacy compliance education

## 7. AMENDMENT AND REVIEW

This Charter may be amended by the Board of Managers. The Charter shall be reviewed annually and updated to reflect technological advancements and security threats.
